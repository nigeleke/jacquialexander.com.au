    • I put off making this decision for some time as I have been concerned at leaving you all with no singing teacher in the area. However, recent moves have meant that I now have two Singing Studios that I can highly recommend.

    Jessica Sibrava

    Milton https://www.facebook.com/jsibravasoprano/

    Jess is a lovely teacher and has a wealth of experience and knowledge. Her classes are similar to mine insomuch as they are private lessons with a focus on technique while the learning is still fun.

    South Coast Singing & Music

    Ulladulla https://www.southcoastsingingandmusic.com/

    Hanna & Bec are also lovely teachers and run a full music school in Ulladulla. They do private lessons and group classes which are good for young students.
