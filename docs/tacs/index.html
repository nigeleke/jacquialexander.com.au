    Suitable for students committed to singing at a discounted rate for full term payments (conditions apply). Part term is at casual rate or full term can be paid and make-up lessons had where time is available.

    Fees: $35 / ½ hour private lesson, paid by term in advance. (usually 10 or 11 weeks)

    In the event that a student is unable to attend a lesson, a make-up lesson may be arranged provided that a vacancy exists and a minimum of five (5) working days cancellation notice has been received with the exception of illness. Non-attendance at a make-up lesson will be deemed to be an attendance. N.B. Make-up lessons cannot be transferred to another time or day.

    Enrolment

    An enrolment form, signed with agreement of conditions, is required on or before the first lesson.

    Term Dates

    Same as Public School Terms (See Schools NSW) No lessons on School Holidays, Pupil Free Days (public schools) or Public Holidays.

    Resources

    A student is required to provide all resources for private use outside lessons, e.g. Original sheet music, original songbooks, CD’s or Mp3’s for backing music, USB or CD’s for recording lessons.

    Payment

    Term-based payment is due on or by the second lesson of each term.

    Casual/Adult payment is due at the time of the lesson.

    Payment by Cash, Cheque, Money Order or Direct Deposit (See invoice for bank a/c details)

    A student will not be permitted to attend lessons without payment of fees.

    Refunds

    Fees are not refundable. Lessons can be transferred to another person in the same term.
